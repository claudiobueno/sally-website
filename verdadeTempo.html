da verdade e do tempo

of truth and time
  • 2020
  • 3:38 min
  • Video Art (black and white, sound)

The present work arises from the proposal of self-reflection and self-representation: self, path, memory. A work with a very personal character, and at the same time opens space for dialogues about self-reflection and possible masks to represent who we are.

Despite a materialized project in the audiovisual field, his idea started in writing, mostly thoughts about: what made me be me? And with that he concludes what he would like to represent with two phrases: “I am the truth that I believe”, represented through images of nature, in this case: the sea - I am part of a divine nature— and “I am made of time: dasmemories and the marks of what I have already lived ”, with images that represent the passage of time, the marks left and small cutouts of specific memories.
