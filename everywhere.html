everywhere

  • 2020
  • 2:42 min
  • Short Film (color, sound)

Everywhere it was directed and produced by the artist Sally Santiago, which was based on the poem written by her husband Claudio Bueno.

The aesthetics and ordinary scenario in the video comes to portray the encounter between God and his creation in the most genuine and simple way. As if the film left your mind telling a little about its own truths.
